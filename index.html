01. About Me

I'm Depavath Ratna, a 3rd Year (6th Semester) B.Tech CSE (Data Science) student at CMR Institute of Technology, Hyderabad. I'm passionate about building tools that automate tedious tasks and make technology more accessible.

My philosophy is simple: "Development is my hobby, not my work." I believe every development makes a part of your problems - the problems make you perfect, so development is nature. I care more about what works than what trends.

My interests span across Software Development, Data Science, Machine Learning, and AI. I enjoy building full-stack applications, writing automation scripts, testing & debugging systems, and creating tools that people actually use. With strong foundations in Python, Java, and C++, I'm always exploring new technologies - from cloud platforms to blockchain to browser extensions. I love working with Linux/Ubuntu, contributing to open-source, and deploying projects on VPS, Docker, and cloud platforms.
